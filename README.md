PlainTalk is a simple and calming platform designed to support people living with dementia in their daily routines. It’s built around clarity, routine, and ease of use, with separate interfaces for caregivers and patients.

Key Features

Caregiver Dashboard
A clean, structured space where caregivers can add and update essential information such as daily notes, reminders, schedules, and personal details. Everything is optimized to be quick to edit and easy to manage.

Patient Interface
A simplified, distraction-free view built to reduce cognitive load. Patients can easily access:

Music – Familiar songs to help with memory recall and comfort.

Photos – Meaningful images that can spark recognition and connection.

Activities – Simple, guided tasks to support daily engagement.

News – Light, readable updates to stay connected with the world.

“Call Help” Button – A large, clearly visible button for immediate assistance.

Personalization
Content can be tailored to each individual’s needs, routines, and preferences, helping the platform feel familiar and supportive rather than overwhelming.

Human-Centered Design

Throughout development, we worked closely with people living with dementia and their family members. Their stories, feedback, and lived experiences shaped the interface, language, and feature priorities—ensuring PlainTalk is grounded in empathy and real-world needs.
